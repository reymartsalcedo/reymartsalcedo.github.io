About Me

I am an active member of the Mathematical Society of the Philippines (MSP), the Southeast Asian Mathematical Society (SEAMS), the Philippine Society for Mathematical Biology (PSMB), and the European Society for Mathematical and Theoretical Biology (ESMTB).

I am originally from the Philippines in which naming conventions follow a Spanish-American blend: Given Names + Mother's Maiden Surname + Father's Surname. Thus, my full legal name is Reymart Salcedo Lagunero. I go just by the name Reymart Salcedo in nonlegally binding settings.
